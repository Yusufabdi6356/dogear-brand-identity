THE IDEA

Bookstores sell books. dogear sells the habit.

1

The problem

People love buying books and struggle to read them. A bookstore's real competitor isn't Amazon — it's the phone at 9pm.

2

The insight

Habit apps solved this with streaks, mascots, and tiny daily wins. Nobody has applied that psychology to a bookstore.

3

The brand

dogear: read a little daily, keep your streak, earn real discounts. Folio — the dog with the folded ear — keeps score.
