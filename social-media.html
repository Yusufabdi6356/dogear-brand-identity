07 · FEED RHYTHM — GREEN / WHITE / DARK / YELLOW ROTATION

The 3-post rhythm

Rotate green → white → dark → yellow so the profile grid reads as a pattern. Never two dark posts in a row. Folio appears at least every third post.

Type does the shouting

One Fredoka headline per post, as big as it can go. Everything else is small Nunito. If the headline needs a paragraph to work, it's not the headline.

Voice: cheerful menace

Duolingo taught the internet that mascots can tease. Folio encourages, guilt-trips gently, and never punches down. Specific beats clever.
