SINCE 2019 · ELM STREET

A bookstore with a dog's loyalty.

dogear started as three bookshelves and a rescue dog named Folio who slept in the window. The shelves multiplied. The dog became a logo. The idea never changed: help people read more of what they love, every single day.

📖

Read it first

Nothing gets a shelf-talker we didn't write ourselves. Every staff pick was actually read, actually loved.

☀️

Habits over hauls

We'd rather you read ten minutes daily than buy ten books yearly. The streak exists because habits beat hype.

🐾

Everyone's shop

Kids' corner, big-print shelf, pay-what-you-can bin, and a water bowl by the door. All readers welcome. All dogs too.
