QUESTIONS

Fair questions

What counts as “reading”?

Anything with pages: novels, comics, cookbooks, poetry. We're not the reading police — if you turned pages and enjoyed it, log it.

What if I get sick or go camping?

One streak freeze per month, automatic. Longer break? Email us — humans answer, and humans understand camping.

Does audio count?

Absolutely. Minutes listened log the same as pages read.
